# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Provide actionable technical feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Disruptive or hostile communication.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or limit participation for behavior that violates this code.
